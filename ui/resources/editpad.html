To get started with symmetry, click on the center tile of your symmetric map and click the Set symmetry plane button. You can change the symmetry plane anytime.

To refresh the map symmetry press the symmetrize button. The contents of the map on the red side will be erased and replaced with the contents on the green side of the map.
